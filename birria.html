Birria

Description

Birria is a Mexican meat dish made from beef. It is characterized by a taste of tomatoes and chili.

Ingredients:
  • Beef
  • Tomato
  • Chili
  • Water
  • Salt
Steps:
  1. Put beef in pot with water chili and salt
  2. Cook for a few hours
  3. Taste and add seasonings
  4. Eat
